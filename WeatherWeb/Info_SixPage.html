WeatherLand

        
Daily Forecast


        Displays the weather forecast for the upcoming days.
        Includes expected temperatures, precipitation, wind, and other relevant data.
        Helps users plan for the day or week ahead.


        
Hourly Forecast


        Provides more granular weather information for each hour of the day.
        Includes data on temperature,RealFeel temperature.


        
24-Hour Detailed Forecast (Every 3 Hours)


        Breaks the day into 3-hour intervals, offering detailed weather conditions for each.
        Includes:
            Temperature
            Wind speed and direction
            Precipitation chances
            Cloud cover
            Humidity
            Pressure
            Visibility


        
Air Pollution


        Displays the current air quality index (AQI).
        Shows levels of pollutants like CO2, NO2, O3, particulate matter (PM2.5 and PM10).

        
Radar


        Clouds
        Displays real-time or near real-time cloud movement.
        Helps users track cloud cover and potential storms.

        Precipitation
        Shows rainfall (or snowfall) intensity and distribution.
        Indicates areas of light to heavy precipitation.

        Sea Level
        Displays current sea level data.
        Could show variations in sea level due to tides or storms.
        Helps monitor coastal conditions and possible flooding.

        Wind Speed
        Shows current wind speeds across regions.

        Temperature
        Provides real-time temperature readings.
        Helps track temperature changes across different locations.
